 
# IELTS Writing Task 2 Template 
 
## 1?? Introduction (2-3 sentences) 
In contemporary society, there is an ongoing debate about whether [改写题目]. 
While some people argue that [观点A], others believe that [观点B]. 
From my perspective, I believe that [我的观点]. 
 
## 2?? Body Paragraph 1 (Main Point) 
Firstly, [观点]. 
This is because [解释]. 
For example, [具体例子]. 
Therefore, [总结]. 
 
## 3?? Body Paragraph 2 (Counter-argument / Concession) 
However, / On the other hand, [对立观点]. 
Although [让步句], [主观点]. 
This is because [原因]. 
For instance, [例子]. 
As a result, [结果]. 
 
## 4?? Conclusion (2 sentences) 
In conclusion, although both sides have their advantages, I believe that [重申观点]. 
In the long run, this approach is more beneficial.
